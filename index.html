Our Story

A Saloon Steeped
in History

More than a restaurant — a living piece of Wyoming heritage.

In the shadow of the Bighorn Mountains, along historic Highway 16, sits a saloon that has been the beating heart of Ten Sleep for generations. With a population of just 249, every guest who walks through our doors becomes part of our story.

Under refreshed new management, the Saloon & Steakhouse carries forward the spirit of the West — from the history on our walls to the hand-cut steaks on your plate. We're more than a stop along the road. We're the destination.
